About

The railway assistant application is designed to streamline the work of train drivers, offering a comprehensive set of tools to enhance their performance and safety. It provides essential aids, including detailed railway line profiles, real-time route warnings, and precise station location information.

By leveraging this app, train drivers can make informed decisions, improve operational efficiency, and ensure a smoother and safer journey for passengers and cargo alike.
